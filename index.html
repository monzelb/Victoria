About me

 

Victoria Chong Der is a professional writer and producer currently employed at Voltage Entertainment. She directs the vision of the Sin with Me series on the Lovestruck app, outlining and plotting 30+ seasons. She has written scripts, collaborated in multiple writer’s rooms, and edited hundreds of pages. Victoria has a B.A. in Creative Writing from Oberlin College and a M.A in Leadership & Management from SIT Graduate Institute.
